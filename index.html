Quick Tips for Creating Great Recruiting Content

  • How to Use This Tool: Select or type a recruiting topic, choose your tone (e.g., Professional & Aspirational or Motivational), and preferred length. Click Generate — the AI will create a compelling draft optimized for attracting experienced loan officers.
  • Choose a Relevant Topic: Focus on what loan officers actually care about — earnings potential, support systems, team culture, work-life balance, growth opportunities, and local market advantages. Strong topics include "How Our Loan Officers Are Scaling to 150+ Loans" or "Why Top Producers Are Joining Our Team in Fort Wayne."
  • Keep It Engaging and Authentic: Aim for 1,000–1,800 words for blog posts. Start with a strong hook, use clear subheadings, real-sounding testimonials, and benefit-driven bullet points. Always include a soft but clear CTA like "Ready to take the next step in your career? Reach out — we’d love to talk."
  • Best Practices After Generation: Always review and personalize the content. Add your own stories, specific team wins, or real examples from your market. This makes the content feel genuine and builds trust with prospective loan officers. Double-check for compliance — never make unrealistic income guarantees.
  • Publish and Promote: Post the blog on your website, then immediately create a Google Business Profile post and social media captions using the generated sections. Share on LinkedIn, Facebook, and industry groups. Reply to every comment — engagement from active loan officers is pure gold.

Why Newsletters Are Pure Gold 💰

Stay Top-of-Mind: Consistent touchpoints = more referrals without cold outreach.

Build Trust: Mix value (market updates, tips) with personal (life updates) = genuine relationships.

People Love Them: Recipes, local events, fun facts — your audience actually opens and enjoys these.

Pro Tips:

  • Send consistently (weekly or monthly) — momentum compounds.
  • Keep it short & scannable — bold headers, emojis, short paragraphs.
  • End with a soft CTA: "Know anyone thinking about buying/refinancing? I'm here to help!"
  • Use tools like Mailchimp/Constant Contact for pretty delivery & tracking.
  • Personal updates are magic — share wins, family, hobbies to humanize yourself.
